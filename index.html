Our goal is to provide a high-quality and affordable pet grooming service for our clients, Petmalu has been working tirelessly to meet their needs and create a memorable experience for our customers. Our goal is to create a place where our pets feel safe, loved, and at ease, while also offering the best possible care.




Our Services

🧼Bathing


A soothing bath with pet-friendly shampoos and conditioners to cleanse and refresh your pet’s coat. We tailor each wash to meet the needs of your pet’s skin and fur type—leaving them clean, soft, and smelling fantastic!

✂️Haircuts


Whether your pet needs a light trim or a stylish new look, we’ve got it covered! Our groomers specialize in breed-specific cuts and custom styles, ensuring your pet feels comfortable and looks their best.

💅Nail Clipping


Proper nail care is essential to your pet's health and comfort. Our groomers carefully trim nails to the perfect length, preventing discomfort and ensuring safe movement—no more clicking claws on your floors!


Why choose us?


✅ Gentle, stress-free grooming environment
✅ Customized care for each pet's unique needs
✅ Trained professionals with a passion for pets
